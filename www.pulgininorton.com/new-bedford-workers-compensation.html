Protecting the Rights of Workplace Injury Victims

Given the choice, most people would probably prefer to be at work earning their full wage rather than being laid up at home with an injury. When an on-the-job injury is serious enough that it keeps you from working, however, it is the employer’s responsibility to provide the necessary workers’ compensation benefits while you recover.

The insurance companies that cover workers’ compensation do not make money when they pay out on claims. Insurance companies make money when they successfully deny them. They will argue that the injury was not work related and do anything they can to find a reason to avoid paying your claim. If you or a loved one has been injured on the job, be sure that you have an experienced workers’ compensation lawyer with the experience to protect your rights.

Ready to Help You With Any Workers’ Compensation Claim

The New Bedford workers’ compensation attorneys at the law firm of Pulgini & Norton, LLP, represent clients in all types of complex on-the-job injury claims. We understand how overwhelming it can be to face unfamiliar legal issues in the wake of a serious injury.   • Knee injuries
  • Joint injuries

  • Work-related heart attacks and strokes
